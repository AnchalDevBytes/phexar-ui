### Spring Checkbox

A playful checkbox with a spring animation. When clicked, it scales down slightly ("squish" effect) and the checkmark springs into view. Ideal for forms or settings pages.

### Task List Checkbox

Designed specifically for to-do lists. When checked, it applies a `line-through` style to the label, providing immediate visual feedback that a task is complete.
